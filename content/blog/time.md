Life, in its fast-paced and fast-changing nature, almost makes one a victim of mindless activities and distractions, which are lost moments we can never regain. Time is a limited asset, arguably every human's most priceless one, and when it is lost once, it never returns. Therefore, it is quite strange that usually we prefer to waste this priceless commodity on trivial stuff. In the last year, it came to my knowledge that too much use of YouTube and gaming had been taken over a considerable time of my days. However, it was only when I decided to work hard and express my thoughts through self-reflection that I confirmed the need to stop such time-wasting activities. Learning to accept the present is a focus that has been put aside so that I can live a life that is more enjoyable and satisfying. Whether you spend too much time on gaming, watching TV, or scrolling on social media, there is always a way out.

## Life is Fleeting

Life’s finite and the impermanence is an undeniable truth. The rapidly changing time provides us with the chance to build the precious moments, make new friends, and realise purposes of our life. The truth is, we are not doing ourselves a favour when we spend our precious time on pointless activates that add little to our self-development or happiness. Face the present and take the best out of every moment.

Time is like currency: you can invest it or waste it. Each of us has a limited amount of time in a day, so we should use it effectively so as to seize many possibilities. Earning a new skill, pursuing a passion project, or building relationships are just a few of the ways moment gives us a chance to make our life richer and more diverse.

Whether we have many hours a day or only a few minutes a day, it isn't about the number of hours but about how we use them. By shifting our emphasis from quantity to quality we can appreciate the fine texture of life's exquisite arrangement. Join making fun and interesting activities that give joy and fulfilment. Be intentional with using your time, and that is the time when all the deep investments you make in meaningful pursuits are the ones that linger a long time.

Affecting many people is the thing that happens when they realise they had to use their time better. Select your focus to be exactly on what is important to you, and your life would be a life filled with days of fulfilment. While I'm not quite there yet, I make an effort to infuse each day with purpose. Whether it involves typing a blog post (as I am doing right now) or dedicating time to study, I strive to ensure that every day contributes meaningfully to my progress and personal growth.

## Be More

It is not enough to leave a lasting legacy by just influencing people; it is a process of admitting and accepting the uniqueness in us and challenging normality. Why live a boring life, barely meeting your academic requirements when you could use all your abilities get you anywhere you want? At the beginning of the exploration, you could consider doing a different hobby, learning a new language, or even writing a book.

Each one of us already has their own gift but often we fail to use it. Through cutting out all these useless distractions and focusing on present times while maintaining a creative point of view, once can have an access to fresh ideas and new solutions in replacing of common ones. Perhaps through the exploration of various hobbies and activities, you might stumble upon an interest that ignites a spark within you—an undiscovered gift waiting to be unveiled. Your own perception and creative input can give the strength to construct a legacy which is different and unique. You could possibly hold the music's highest honour in your hands as a Grammy or the a nobel prise in the future if you tried hard enough. Who knows? There's only one way to find out.

Forget the norm and live the life of an extraordinary one by practising this as a guiding principle. Question all the expected highest status and the standard practise; be courageous and opt for non-mainstream choices and paths. The usual pattern is that it is the overly unexceptional people that leave the world with an insignificant trace. Release yourself from the chains of convention that will tarnish your legacy. Create a mark that will make you stand out.

## The Best Time to Start is Now

Waiting for the perfect moment is a never-ending loop. The important thing is to take the first step now. Whether it's a new project, a skill you want to learn, or building relationships with new people—waiting for the perfect moment to start is just a waste of even more time, because that moment will never come. Wear a smile, just do it, and allow yourself to have this ride. In truth, the perfect timing is a legend; your power stems from the presence. No waiting, start now and create the lifestyle you have been dreaming of. 

As you reach the end of this blog post, resist the urge mindlessly scroll through TikTok afterwards or waste your valuable time. This is your chance—take it as a call to action: a moment to start, to take control of yourself, and to spend every future moment towards a lifetime filled with purpose. Your journey begins now.
