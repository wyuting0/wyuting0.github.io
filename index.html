Accomplishments

I'm proud to have achieved AL6D for the PSLE, showcasing my dedication and hard work. Despite not meeting my initial expectations, it allowed me to enroll in Hwa Chong Institution, which, though may not be my dream school (Raffles Institution), is a pretty prestigious school. This accomplishment has boosted my confidence and determination for future academic challenges.

My English essay writing improved a lot from primary 4 to primary 6. I used to get around 20/40, but by the time I reached primary 6, my scores were consistently above 35/40. I achieved this by watching a lot of YouTube videos and reading a lot of books. The videos helped me understand writing techniques and grammar, while reading different books exposed me to various writing styles. It's proof that a mix of videos and books can make a big difference in learning and improving language skills.

Getting into the Gifted Education Programme during Primary 3 was a big deal for me. At first, I wasn't sure if I'd make it, but I worked really hard on my maths and English. In the end, it paid off. Along the way, I not only got better at these subjects but also learned how to think more effectively and manage my time wisely. It taught me that working hard and staying consistent is important when preparing for tests.
